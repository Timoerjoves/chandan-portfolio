About EduBuddy

EduBuddy is a smart academic assistant built to help students from 8th to 10th grade stay focused, manage their studies, and receive AI-driven learning support. From personalized task planners to doubt-solving, flashcards, reports, and YouTube learning integrations — EduBuddy makes learning feel guided, adaptive, and effortless.

Designed for students across India with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between scattered study habits and structured, productive learning without overwhelming the learner.
