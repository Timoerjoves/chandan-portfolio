About AI Rice Mill

This project is a smart automation system for rice mills. We’ve built a powerful backend that processes truck entries, lab report data, and intelligently classifies rice grades using AI. Our system eliminates manual errors, improves decision-making, and delivers insights via an intuitive dashboard.

Designed with scalability and operational ease in mind, it showcases how AI can streamline traditional industries through clean design and practical logic.
